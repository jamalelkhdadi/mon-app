Tutorials Writing for Us

Are you exceptionally good at creating self-learning material in attractive layout? If the answer is yes, then we have a golden opportunity for you to work with us as a freelancer and earn a handsome salary by producing high-quality tutorials for us. We are specifically looking for people having sufficient domain knowledge in the following areas:

  • Information Technology

  • Software Quality management

  • Java technologies

  • Mainframe technologies

  • Web development technologies

  • Project Management

  • Accounting and Finance

  • Telecommunication

  • Big Data

  • Microsoft Technologies

  • Business Intelligence

  • SAP Modules

  • Open Sources

  • Soft Skills

  • Academic Subjects from Engineering and Management Syllabus

  • Any other subject where you can write great content....

How It Works?

First of all, you are required to produce a sample write-up of 2-3 pages to show your writing capabilities and command over the chosen subject.

If you qualify based on the provided sample write-up, we will move on with the following steps:

  • We will work with you to prepare a Table of Content (ToC) for the subject on which you are going to write a tutorial.

  • Based on the finalized ToC, you as the author will give us a proposal indicating the delivery plan and the total cost of the tutorial, taking into account the time and effort required to produce the final draft.

  • We will then have a discussion with you and finalize the cost of the tutorial along with its tentative delivery plan.

  • Thereafter you start developing the tutorial and submit your draft for a technical and presentation review.

  • Tutorials Point will provide necessary comments and feedback which you will incorporate and submit the final draft of the tutorial.

  • Once you deliver the final output with the desired quality, we will release your payment either through bank-to-bank transaction or through Paypal.

  • There is also an arrangement to divide the whole payment in different installments based on content delivery milestones.

Content Copyright

Once a tutorial is accepted and published on our website tutorialspoint.com, Tutorials Point India Limited holds the complete copyright of the content and the same content cannot be re-used by the author for any further online or offline publishing.

We respect our tutorial writers and assume they are great subject matter experts. We expect them to write their tutorials based on their experience and expertise only. We strictly follow copyright laws and hence, we do not accept any content which has been copied from any other websites, books, or other copyright-protected resources.

How Much You Can Earn?

It very much depends on various factors such as the length of the tutorial, its complexity, popularity of the subject, etc. but in general we pay in the range of $250 to $500 per tutorial. If you are good at writing and deliver as per the standards set by Tutorials Point, then you will be assigned another tutorial immediately after completing one. So there is no waiting time and no limit to how much you can earn.

How to Apply?

Send your profile along with some of your sample work to contact@tutorialspoint.com mentioning the subject on which you want to write. Please note we pay a one-time fixed cost per tutorial. No royalty or any other incentives will be paid to the author for the delivered content.
